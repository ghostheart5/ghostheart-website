Signal 01

Rise Again

Love • Strength • Defiance • Partnership

The Meaning

Some people survive because they are strong.

Some survive because someone refuses to let them fall alone.

Rise Again is a signal about standing together against the world.

Not because life became easier.

Not because the road cleared.

But because when everything said "give up," we chose to keep going.

Every setback became another reason to rise.

Every scar became proof that we were still here.

She is the shield.

Signal 03

I Still Would

Family • Grief • Memory • Legacy

The Meaning

Some people leave this world.

But they never truly leave our lives.

They remain in traditions.

In conversations we still hear.

In places we still visit.

In memories that refuse to fade.

I Still Would is about the people I wish were still here.

The family I miss.

The moments I would relive.

The traditions I would still keep.

The calls I would still answer.

The memories I would still make.

If they were here today...

I still would.

Some people leave this world.
The love never does.
- GhostHeart

Signal 04

I Hate You Daddy

Survival • Abandonment • Strength • Motherhood

The Meaning

Some wounds come from what happened.

Some come from who never showed up.

I Hate You Daddy is not just a song about anger.

It's about growing up with unanswered questions.

It's about wondering why someone who should have been there wasn't.

It's about being raised by a mother who carried burdens that were never supposed to be hers alone.

This signal is for every child who learned to survive without the person who was supposed to stay.

It's about pain.

It's about absence.

But most of all...

It's about becoming something anyway.

Momma made me a man.
Daddy didn't do a damn.
- GhostHeart
